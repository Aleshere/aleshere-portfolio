About Me

Some people get out of school ans start working knowing exactly where to be in the near future.
I was never like that.
I started travelling instead and came across many cultures and people. I worked jobs, and saw places, and learned languages, and grew amazing memories that will be with me for the rest of my life. Then one day I met my wife to be, and she encouraged me follow my passion and start coding... and from that day I never stopped. I learned the basics, improved my knowledge, stumbled across obstacles and solved them, and ultimately self taught myself a profession.

Years later here I am: a reliable, helpful, friendly web developer ready to help you!
